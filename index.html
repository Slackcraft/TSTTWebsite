About the Book

My book is written in memory of all those victims, whose lives have been taken away by domestic violence. Both here in Australia and Africa.

My purpose is to inspire other women, men and children, who suffer at the hands of Domestic Violence – they too can become survivors!

I will donate all proceeds of this book, “Ubuntu - Smoke that Thunders” to various children's shelter and welfare charity organizations and ensure that every cent is used with wisdom - to ensure that all Zimbabwe’s orphans will receive food, shelter, clothing and the love that they so truly deserve. This is my pledge.
